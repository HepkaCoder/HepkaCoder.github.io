How to use

The Robux Tax Calculator helps you figure out how much Robux you actually get after Roblox takes its cut from things like game passes, shirts, or other items you sell.

There are two modes you can choose from: Before Taxes and After Taxes.

Before Taxes tells you how much you should price your item for if you want to earn a specific amount after Roblox’s tax.

After Taxes shows how much Robux you’ll actually receive if your item is priced at the amount you entered.
